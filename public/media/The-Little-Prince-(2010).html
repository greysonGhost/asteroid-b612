The Little Prince ran from 2010 to 2015/16 and got three seasons for a total of 36 episodes. Each episode/story aired as 2-3 episodes for a total of 78 episodes. We don't have synopses of all the episodes because this show is impossible to find online and I don't even speak French. We do know all episode titles and planet numbers. Since there is a comic adapation of the first two seasons we can safely assume those plots would be the same or at least similar.

Lost Media Status:

Pretty much considered lost media at this point. There are parts of episodes up on YouTube but nowhere near the full show, not even a full season. You can find DVDs for some episodes (I have episodes 1 + 2 on DVD (Planet of Time and Planet of the Firebird)) but nobody is uploading them anywhere as far as I can tell.

It's a bit difficult to write a wiki for a show you can't even watch properly.
