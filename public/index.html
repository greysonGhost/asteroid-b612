Asteroid B-612
an unoffical wiki / fansite for the book the little prince and many of its adaptations

The Little Prince is a novella written and illustrated by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry and was published in 1943. Since it's publications it has become a world renowned classic and has been the basis for many films, tv shows, graphic novels, etc. This site hopes to serves as a sort of hub for everything The Little Prince related and hopes to be helpful for anyone wanting a compiled collection of information about the original work or any of its adaptations.

This site is still very much a work in progress. You can view the avaiable pages with content on the sitemap. If you'd like to contribute you can submit a pull request over on github.

This site is fan-made and is not affiliated with any of the The Little Prince properties or Antoine de Saint-Exupéry
